BRAVE THE FEAR | History of the Case

“The population is resigned to the evils of the leaders of Burundi, citizens do not express themselves for fear of being killed, we call on Burundians to
brave the fear.”

Floriane Irangabiye - reported to be the only female journalist imprisoned on the continent of Africa - has been imprisoned for a year and three months on charges of "undermining the integrity of the national territory".
Sentenced to 10 years in prison and a fine of $1 million Burundian francs. View a timeline of her case below.
